Tell your work coach your history

This tool collects information about you and your work history that will make your work coach meeting more useful and tailored to you.

It should take around 15 minutes to complete.

You’ll be asked about:

  • your work or unemployment for the last 2 years
  • any training or education you’ve taken

You’ll be able to print out or email your answers at the end to give to your work coach.

Your answers will not be stored or saved.
